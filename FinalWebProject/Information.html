Uses For Ducks

Some people might think that rubber ducks are only for kids. Well thats a lie. There are many reasons as to why you could own a rubber duck. Some people have them as keepsakes, while others use them in their jobs. How would someone use them in their job you might ask. Well, there is a debugging method for coders called rubber duck debugging. Its where the coder takes a rubber duck and explains what the code is doing to help the coder find the error in the program. Not only that but some work places have ducks for each person that makes sound in the office.
